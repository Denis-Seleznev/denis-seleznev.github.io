Zombie arena

For the second project I decided to create something more active. During creation I used more game object components. Also, I worked a bit with animations and created simple UI.

Difficulties: One difficulty was that at first I used MoveTowards() method to make zombies follow the player. That caused them to push the player through walls from time to time. I coped with that by calculating vectors and modifying zombies' velocity instead of changing their transform component directly (what MoveTowards() did). That would also make zombies slide away if the player had bumped into them. At first, I didn't like that effect, but after modifying zombies' mass, it looked like a cool "push away" mechanics.
