Zombie arena

For the second project I decided to create something more active, but yet easy for a beginner. During creation I used more game object components. Also, I worked a bit with animations and created simple UI.

Difficulties: One difficulty was that at first I used MoveTowards() method to make zombies follow the player. That caused them to push the player through walls from time to time. I coped with that by calculating vectors and modifying zombies' velocity instead of changing zombie's transform component (which MoveTowards() did). That would also make zombies slide away if the player had bumped into them. At first, I didn't like that effect, but then, after modifying their mass, it started to look like a cool "push away" mechanics.
